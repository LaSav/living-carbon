Living Carbon is a new for-profit social and environmental impact company that targets compelling financial, social and environmental returns.

We carefully select, develop and operate large, multi-faceted carbon offset projects in Africa and Asia: reforestation, soil regeneration and clean energy (fuel switching), generating premium value carbon credits that are sold long term to global corporates to complete their transition to net zero.

Living Carbon operates in alignment with eleven of the UN’s sustainable development goals.

  • Assist developed world businesses to complete the transition to net zero.
  • Generate long term rental incomes from carbon credits.
  • Invest in community development in rural Africa and Asia.
  • Create sustainable agricultural income streams and associated local livelihoods.
  • Aggregate, preserve and rewild vast swathes of landscape − enhancing critical global carbon sinks.
  • Alleviate rural poverty in Africa and Asia at scale – a developmental goal that has alluded governments to date.
