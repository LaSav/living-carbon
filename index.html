Our Mission

Lower Global Carbon Emissions

From wildfires and heatwaves to droughts and floods, the effects of rising levels of greenhouse gases in the planet's atmosphere are increasingly evident. Living Carbon develops reliable and quantifiable offset projects, underpinned by technology and transparency, to support organisations in their efforts to reach carbon neutrality.
